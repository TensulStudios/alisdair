About Me

I'm a 14-year-old game developer from Scotland with a passion for creating immersive digital experiences. I work with HTML5 and CSS to build web-based projects, and I'm currently learning C# to expand my skillset into more advanced game development.

Music is a huge part of my creative process. My favourite artists include Hixor, EsDeeKid, FakeMink, 9090Gate, Feng, and Fimiguerrero—their sound inspires a lot of my work and keeps me motivated.

My goal is simple: become one of the best developers out there. I'm constantly learning, experimenting, and pushing myself to create better projects every day.
